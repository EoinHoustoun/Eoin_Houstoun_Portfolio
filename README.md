## Project Highlights

### 1. Alzheimer's Disease Classification: *Published at AIAI 2025, Springer*

Classified individuals into cognitive states of Alzheimer's disease using ML on the Bio-Hermes digital biomarker dataset. Lead author of peer-reviewed paper published in Springer's *Artificial Intelligence Applications and Innovations*.

- Models: XGBoost, Random Forest, Monte Carlo cross-validation, feature selection
- [View Paper on Springer](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-031-96235-6_5) · [View Repo](https://github.com/EoinHoustoun/Alzheimers_Biohermes)

<img src="docs/assets/Research_Image.png" width="420"/>

---

### 2. Fever Prediction with Infrared Thermography

Regression and classification models estimating oral temperature and fever status (≥ 37.5 °C) from non-contact thermal sensor data, with SHAP explainability throughout.

- Models: Custom PyTorch MLPs, XGBoost, nested cross-validation, SHAP analysis
- [View Repo](https://github.com/EoinHoustoun/Infrared_Thermography)

<img src="docs/assets/infra.png" width="480"/>

---

### 3. Pediatric Appendicitis Prediction

Clinical decision-support tool predicting pediatric appendicitis diagnosis and severity from clinical, laboratory, and ultrasound data. Deployed as a live Gradio web app.

- Models: XGBoost, MLP neural networks, SHAP, Monte Carlo CV, Gradio deployment
- [View Repo](https://github.com/EoinHoustoun/Pediatric_Appendicitis)

<img src="docs/assets/poster.png" width="420"/>

---

### 4. Neural Style Transfer: Generative AI

Transforms celebrity photographs into comic book illustrations using deep neural artistic rendering, combining content and style loss functions.

- Models: VGG19, PyTorch CNN, neural artistic rendering
- [View Repo](https://github.com/EoinHoustoun/Generative_AI)

![Comic Book Style Transfer](https://github.com/EoinHoustoun/Eoin_Houstoun/blob/master/style_transfer6.gif?raw=true)

---

### 5. AI Chef Chatbot with NLP

Interactive recipe recommendation chatbot with a Tkinter desktop GUI using DistilBERT for semantic similarity and TF-IDF retrieval.

- Models: DistilBERT, TF-IDF, cosine similarity, web scraping (BeautifulSoup)
- [View Repo](https://github.com/EoinHoustoun/AI_Chef)

<img src="docs/assets/ai_chef.png" width="480"/>

---

### 6. Validation Procedures Exploration

Football player transfer fee prediction as a rigorous case study for ML validation methodology, systematically comparing cross-validation strategies.

- Techniques: Regression, k-fold CV, Monte Carlo CV, temporal validation, bootstrapping
- [View Repo](https://github.com/EoinHoustoun/Final-Year-Project)

<img src="docs/assets/int_val.png" width="420"/>

### 7. Amazon Recommendation System

Collaborative and content-based filtering for personalised product recommendations at scale.

---

### 8. Retail Customer Prediction

Customer purchase propensity modelling using classification ensembles for a retail environment.

### 9. Customer Segmentation

Unsupervised clustering to identify distinct customer personas for targeted marketing strategy.
